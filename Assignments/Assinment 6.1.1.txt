4. Write a Python program to construct the following pattern, using a nested for loop. 
*  
* *  
* * *  
* * * *  
* * * * *  
* * * *  
* * *  
* *  
* 

num=5
#going forwards
for i in range(num):
    for j in range(i):
        print ('* ', end="")
    print('')
#Peaked and coming back
for i in range(num,0,-1):
    for j in range(i):
        print('* ', end="")
    print('')
